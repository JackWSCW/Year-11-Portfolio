Low Poly Character

OVERVIEW

The following project shows the 3D low poly character that I created in the program blender. I chose to create a tall male character with a muscular frame, and make both an idle and running animation for him.

  • HTML
  • CSS

    END USERS

    My end users are teenagers 13-18 years old who like rapid action/adventure/ fighting games. The character will have details the same as humans so that the user feels like they are the warrior themselves running through the enviroment in the game, the aim is to make it feel realistic. To further acheive this I will include animations similar to real life like realistic fighting moves, and effects, as my end user will likely be looking for effects like this.

    REQUIREMENTS

  • Head

    • The head has to be a realistic shape, meaning round, and a sharp jaw line as it is a muscular character.

  • Arms

    • Arms have to be somewhat large, and include muscles such as the biceps and triceps, as well as large shoulders. The character also have to have moving elbows when it does specific movements.

  • Legs

    • The legs have to be bigger than the average human, meaning muscles like the qauds, and calfs have to be clearly developed on the character. The character also has to have bending knees when it moves for the realism factor.

  • Torso/Chest

    • The wasit on the character has to be slimmer, and abs will be present, above that the pectorial muscles will be large and wider then the waist, as well as stick out further in the front then the stomach, this is to involve masculine traits present in real life, especially refuring to warriors in history.

  • Muscles

    • Throughout the whole character main muscles groups have to be visible and obviously present.

  • Height and General Size

    • The character has to be on the taller side, and have a larger frame.

  • Facial hair

    • The character has to have a mid lenght beard all around their face, about the lenghth of a man's fist in real life

  • Clothing

    • My character has to have the pectorial muscles showing, as well as all of the arms.
